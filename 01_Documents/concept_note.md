# PROJECT CONCEPT NOTE: AI_BIOFRIDGE ECOSYSTEM

## 1. Problem Statement
Current premium smart refrigerators act as passive storage bins. Users must input expiry labels manually, leading to missed items and waste. Furthermore, home electronics lack direct synchronization with human physiological needs, failing to provide proactive support when users are tired or immobilized.

## 2. System Architecture

### Component A: Main Smart Family Refrigerator
- **Primary Function:** Household culinary logistics hub, large capacity preservation, and biometric collection.
- **Core AI Execution:** Continual object classification (computer vision) and real-time biometric filtering (rPPG analysis on skin pixels to monitor resting heart rate trends).
- **Proactive Logic:** Cross-references internal ingredients with active health vitals to curate custom meal plans for the household.

### Component B: Autonomous Mobile Cooling Vehicle
- **Primary Function:** "Last-meter" nutrient transport to the lounge, living room, or bedroom.
- **Core Engineering:** Self-driven mobility utilizing a continuous AC-line tethered to a mechanical cable reel. It acts as an autonomous vehicle that physically brings fresh food and refreshments directly next to a user's seat.
- **User Value:** Delivers true luxury relaxation for busy individuals, senior citizens, or those with mobility limitations by offering an interactive refreshment delivery platform on demand.

## 3. Product Vision & Roadmap
- **Phase 1 (Software Core):** Finalize rPPG signal filtering (FFT/Butterworth) and OCR bounding boxes.
- **Phase 2 (Hardware Integration):** Prototype the motorized mechanical cable spool and sensor arrays for both enclosures.
- **Phase 3 (Ecosystem Integration):** Connect both devices via local wireless protocols for fluid synchronization.
